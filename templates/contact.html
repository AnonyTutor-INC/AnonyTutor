Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a student?
To register as a student, head over to our registration page, enter your information and we will match you up anonymously with a tutor!
When will I be matched?
The amount of time it takes to be matched is largely dependent on the tutors we have, we will work to match you up as soon as possible!
What if I want to switch tutors?
That's no problem! Every session you can choose to be matched with the same or different tutor
